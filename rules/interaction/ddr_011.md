# Open navigation on explicit intent

Navigation menus should open on explicit click rather than incidental hover; hover may reveal secondary local row controls.

## Why

Explicit activation avoids accidental navigation while preserving efficient disclosure of secondary actions.

## Prefer

- Require a click or equivalent explicit activation to open navigation.
- Keep hover effects local and non-navigational.

## Avoid

- Opening a navigation surface because the pointer merely crossed it.

## Use when

- A menu or navigation surface changes place or reveals a substantial panel.

## Do not use when

- A platform-native pattern requires hover disclosure and is safely reversible.

## Evidence

- Required navigation dropdowns to open only after an explicit click and noted the intended behavior still failed.

## Check

- Can pointer movement accidentally open or navigate?
- Is keyboard activation equivalent?
