# Keep a docked agent composer persistent

A docked composer remains present and operable through resize, panel, note, and timeline transitions.

## Why

Docking signals sustained work; losing the composer during ordinary workspace transitions breaks that contract.

## Prefer

- Preserve composer presence, content, and operability across normal workspace transitions.

## Avoid

- Unmounting or disabling the docked composer during adjacent navigation or resize.

## Use when

- The user has explicitly docked a composer for sustained work.

## Do not use when

- The user closes or explicitly undocks it, or a hard permission boundary requires dismissal.

## Evidence

- Reported resizing a side panel temporarily made the docked composer disappear.

## Check

- Does the composer survive resize and navigation?
- Is draft context preserved?
- Can the user still submit or recover?
